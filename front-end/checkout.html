Tutorial - Step 1, Task 2

Now that the main ordering menu has been completed, you will be desiging a menu that allows users to add sides to their orders. You will have a special sides component to give structure to you design.


Remember to keep in mind that you are trying to demonstrate Fitts' Law with your interface!

Tutorial - Step 2, Task 2

Now that your design has been made, it's time to test it out! You will be given a set of items you need to add to an order.


Once you've added all the appropriate items to the order, the timer will stop and you can move onto the third task.
